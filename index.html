Entangled with Flavors

Lemon

Brewing water is filtered a second time through fresh lemon peels. Lemon juices are added sparingly for just the right amount of bittersweet taste.

Grapefruit

Charged with grapefruit – this flavor melds well with our lemons to create a tangy and bitter taste.

Orange

Our cold lager formula is infused with orange juice to help counter the lemon and grapefruit’s bitter tastes – creating a well-balanced beer.

What makes Quantum Craft Different?

Unlike most IPAs, our formula combines malts, hops, and fruits. We’ve crafted a surprisingly smooth beer with a moderate and tangy carbonated aftertaste. Our brewing mountain spring water undergoes a double-filtration process – using lemon peels for a refreshing thirst-quenching taste.

Quantum Craft’s bittersweet taste is further accentuated through our family’s timeless chemical formula. The chloride anion enhances the beer’s sweetness while the right amount of carbonate ions emphasize an equal and opposite bitter taste. It is truly a taste that one has to experience for themselves.
